Your labs, decoded

Stop guessing how you're aging. Know it.

Biomarq takes the labs you already have, glucose, lipids, inflammation, and more, and turns them into an estimated biological age, sharp suggestions for fixing what's off, and honest context on your cardiometabolic, cardiovascular, cognitive, and cancer risk. Lifestyle and nutrition come along for the ride, as support, not the headline.

Biomarkers first
Log glucose, lipids, ApoB, inflammation, and more. Get a biological age estimate and specific, evidence-informed suggestions for each marker outside range.
Lifestyle & nutrition
Track sleep, activity, and a food journal with real macro data, set as supporting context, not the main event.
Disease risk context
See how your markers relate to cardiometabolic, cardiovascular, cognitive, and cancer-risk research, with honest notes on what real screening actually requires.
Not a diagnostic tool. Always talk to a doctor about your results.
